## What Makes This Format Work

✅ **Title:** Specific and action-oriented (not generic "News from PSS")  
✅ **Date:** Machine-readable (YYYY-MM-DD)  
✅ **Summary:** 2-3 sentences max (people scroll; summaries drive clicks)  
✅ **Tags:** Categorical (helps visitors filter by topic/geography/type)  
✅ **Image:** Standardized (16:9 aspect ratio, consistent sizing)  
✅ **Source:** Linked back to LinkedIn (drives engagement, verifiable)

This format matches what's already working on your site and keeps news consistently presented.
